--- D&D 5E CORE MECHANICS ---
ROLLING: Success = d20 + modifiers vs Difficulty Class (DC).
DC GUIDELINES: 5 (Very Easy), 10 (Easy), 15 (Medium), 20 (Hard), 25 (Very Hard).

--- VALIDATION CHECKLIST (AI INSTRUCTIONS) ---
1. HP CHECK: Ensure Max HP matches the Class Hit Die + CON Modifier.
2. ARMOR CHECK: Ensure the character is not wearing armor their class prohibits (e.g., Wizards in Plate).
3. STAT CHECK: Stats should follow standard array logic (Highest ~15-17, Lowest ~8). No stats over 20 at Level 1.
4. BIO CHECK: Ensure the backstory fits a fantasy setting and isn't "god-moding".

--- CLASS RULES (LEVEL 1) ---
(Use this to validate HP, Armor, and Gear)

1. BARBARIAN
   - Hit Die: d12 (HP = 12 + CON mod)
   - Armor: Light, Medium, Shields. (Unarmored Defense allowed).
   - Saves: Strength, Constitution.
   - Illegal Gear: Heavy Armor.

2. BARD
   - Hit Die: d8 (HP = 8 + CON mod)
   - Armor: Light Armor only.
   - Saves: Dexterity, Charisma.
   - Illegal Gear: Medium/Heavy Armor, Shields.

3. CLERIC
   - Hit Die: d8 (HP = 8 + CON mod)
   - Armor: Light, Medium, Shields. (Some domains allow Heavy).
   - Saves: Wisdom, Charisma.
   
4. DRUID
   - Hit Die: d8 (HP = 8 + CON mod)
   - Armor: Light, Medium, Shields.
   - RESTRICTION: Druids will NOT wear armor or use shields made of metal.
   - Saves: Intelligence, Wisdom.

5. FIGHTER
   - Hit Die: d10 (HP = 10 + CON mod)
   - Armor: All Armor (Light, Medium, Heavy), Shields.
   - Saves: Strength, Constitution.

6. MONK
   - Hit Die: d8 (HP = 8 + CON mod)
   - Armor: None. (Unarmored Defense).
   - Saves: Strength, Dexterity.
   - Illegal Gear: Armor, Shields.

7. PALADIN
   - Hit Die: d10 (HP = 10 + CON mod)
   - Armor: All Armor, Shields.
   - Saves: Wisdom, Charisma.

8. RANGER
   - Hit Die: d10 (HP = 10 + CON mod)
   - Armor: Light, Medium, Shields.
   - Saves: Strength, Dexterity.
   - Illegal Gear: Heavy Armor.

9. ROGUE
   - Hit Die: d8 (HP = 8 + CON mod)
   - Armor: Light Armor only.
   - Saves: Dexterity, Intelligence.
   - Illegal Gear: Medium/Heavy Armor, Shields.

10. SORCERER
    - Hit Die: d6 (HP = 6 + CON mod)
    - Armor: None.
    - Saves: Constitution, Charisma.
    - Illegal Gear: Any Armor, Shields.

11. WARLOCK
    - Hit Die: d8 (HP = 8 + CON mod)
    - Armor: Light Armor.
    - Saves: Wisdom, Charisma.
    - Illegal Gear: Medium/Heavy Armor, Shields (unless Hexblade).

12. WIZARD
    - Hit Die: d6 (HP = 6 + CON mod)
    - Armor: None.
    - Saves: Intelligence, Wisdom.
    - Illegal Gear: Any Armor, Shields.

--- COMBAT BASICS ---
- INITIATIVE: d20 + DEX modifier.
- ATTACK ROLL: d20 + Ability Mod + Proficiency Bonus (+2 at Level 1).
- DAMAGE: Weapon Die + Ability Mod.
- DEATH SAVES: 3 successes to stabilize, 3 failures to die. Reset on regain HP.
